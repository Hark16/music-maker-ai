Studio Documentation

Master the tools and techniques of MusicMakerAI with our comprehensive guide.

Welcome to the Future of Sound

Music Maker Studio is a lightweight yet powerful web-based notation editor designed for musicians, beatmakers, and producers. It uses Tone.js to simulate professional-grade synthesizers and drum machines directly in your browser.

1. The Studio Interface

Notation Box

Where your composition lives. Each block (e.g., pc120) represents a note played by an instrument at a specific tempo.

Music Grid

A reactive interface. Clicking a pad triggers a sound and automatically inserts a notation block into the editor.

Master Controls

Play back your sequence, enable Loop Play, export audio, or save your text notation.

2. Understanding Notation

The notation format follows a strict [Prefix][Note][Tempo], structure.

p : Piano pc120,
g : Guitar ge140,
v : Violin vf#100,
d : Drum (1-12) d1, d4,

Drum Sound Key (Pads 1-12)

1: Kick
2: Snare
3: Closed Hi-hat
4: Open Hi-hat
5: Tom 1
6: Tom 2
7: Clap
8: Crash
9: Perc 1
10: Perc 2
11: Shaker
12: Rim Shot

3. Troubleshooting

No Sound?

  • Ensure you clicked "START AUDIO ENGINE" on load.
  • Check if your browser tab is muted.
  • Use Google Chrome or Microsoft Edge for the best experience.
